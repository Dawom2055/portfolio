Systems Design Engineering

What is Systems?

A question that I get asked constantly when I tell someone my Major is what is Systems Design Engineering? What do you learn? What jobs do you get? And my answer to that question is the same everytime. Majoring in Systems Design Engineering gives you the skill sets to solve various technical problem ranging from Software to Hardware. It focuses on the bigger picture and gives you a understanding of how things are connected. It requires a multi-disciplinary approach to learning but in returns helps you become a more versatile problem solver.

What I like to Learn

The thing that really peeks my interest is how things work and what makes them come together. I like to look at the bigger picture and see what is the system that is making this product come together and successful. I am really interested Product Managment which correlates with my interests and provides me with the responsibility for planning, designing, organizing, and directing the completion of specific projects. I love design and creating products which made Systems Design Engineering a perfect place for me.
